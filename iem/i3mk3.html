TRI i3 MK3

A premium tribrid IEM offering a cohesive, balanced sound for audiophiles, gamers, and musicians seeking high-fidelity audio.

Key Features:

  • Tribrid configuration (1DD + 1BA + 1 Planar)
  • 10mm Beryllium diaphragm dynamic driver for bass
  • Impedance: 21Ω ±1 (@1kHz)
  • Sensitivity: 104 ±1dB (@1kHz)
  • Frequency Response: 20Hz–20kHz
  • Sonion 2356 balanced armature for mids
  • 10mm planar magnetic driver for treble
  • 612-core 5N OCC silver-plated coaxial cable (4.4mm balanced plug)
  • CNC-machined aluminum alloy shells with purple anodized finish

Why Choose the TRI i3 MK3?

Priced at $199, the TRI i3 MK3 combines a 10mm Beryllium dynamic driver, Sonion 2356 BA, and a 10mm planar driver for a neutral-warm sound with exceptional clarity and dynamics. Its precise imaging and expansive soundstage make it ideal for gaming and music, delivering punchy bass, lush mids, and electrostatic-like treble. The robust build and rich accessories enhance its value, making it a standout in the competitive $200 IEM market.

More Details:

The TRI i3 MK3’s sound profile is meticulously tuned for balance, as praised in Head-Fi and KEEPHIFI reviews. The Beryllium DD delivers fast, controlled bass with satisfying sub-bass rumble and impactful mid-bass, avoiding bleed into the mids. The Sonion 2356 BA produces smooth, velvety mids with rich vocal texture, excelling in genres like jazz and acoustic. The planar driver offers airy, detailed treble with a luxurious, electrostatic-like quality, ensuring fatigue-free listening, though some note slight brightness in long sessions.

The build quality is exceptional, with lightweight CNC-machined aluminum shells in a striking purple anodized finish, resistant to scratches. The ergonomic design ensures comfort for extended use, though the protruding nozzle may require larger ear tips for some. The 612-core OCC silver-plated cable (4.4mm only) enhances high-frequency clarity but limits compatibility with 3.5mm sources. Accessories include 11 pairs of ear tips (Clarion, balanced, bass, foam), a leather case, cleaning cloth, and brush, though the case could be larger for the thick cable. With 21Ω impedance and 104dB sensitivity, it’s easy to drive with dongles like the FiiO KB3, but shines with powerful sources like the DC04 Pro for maximum dynamics.
