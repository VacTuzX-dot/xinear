JUZEAR x Z Reviews Defiant

A stunningly designed hybrid IEM delivering punchy, balanced sound for audiophiles and casual listeners alike.

Key Features:

  • Quad-driver hybrid configuration (1DD + 3BA)
  • 10mm third-generation dynamic driver with PU composite diaphragm
  • Impedance: 32Ω
  • Sensitivity: 109dB ±1dB/mW
  • Frequency Response: 20Hz–20kHz
  • Premium FLARE cable with 6N SPOCC+SCCW hybrid structure (70 strands per conductor)
  • High-precision DLP 3D-printed resin shells with CNC-milled faceplates
  • Modular termination plugs (3.5mm single-ended, 4.4mm balanced)

Why Choose the JUZEAR x Z Reviews Defiant?

The JUZEAR x Z Reviews Defiant, priced at $99.99, combines a 10mm dynamic driver with three custom balanced armature drivers for a rich, engaging sound. Tuned with Zeos from Z Reviews over six months, it offers controlled bass, clear mids, and smooth treble, making it perfect for music, movies, and gaming. Its vibrant Rainbow or Jade Green CNC-milled faceplates and ergonomic design ensure both style and comfort.

More Details:

The Defiant’s sound profile shines with impactful, well-controlled bass from its 10mm dynamic driver, delivering depth without muddiness, ideal for bass-heavy tracks or cinematic gaming moments. The mids, handled by a composite BA driver, provide natural vocals and instrument clarity, while the custom high-frequency BA ensures crisp, fatigue-free treble. Reviews praise its imaging and soundstage, making it a strong choice for competitive gaming where positional audio is key, like in FPS titles.

The build quality is exceptional, with skin-friendly resin shells and a micro-perforation pressure balance system for long-session comfort. The included FLARE cable, with 280 total strands and interchangeable plugs, adds versatility and premium feel. Accessories include multiple silicone eartips, a faux leather case, and a cleaning cloth, though eartips are noted as generic. Its low impedance and high sensitivity make it easy to drive with smartphones or dongle DACs, scaling well with high-end sources like the iFi Hip DAC 3.
