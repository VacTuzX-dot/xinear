SIMGOT SuperMix 4

A highly versatile quadbrid in-ear monitor (IEM) suitable for gamers, audiophiles, and music enthusiasts seeking immersive sound.

Key Features:

  • Quad-driver hybrid configuration (1DD + 1BA + 1 Planar + 1 PZT)
  • 10mm dynamic driver for powerful bass
  • Impedance: 7.2Ω ±15% (@1kHz)
  • Sensitivity: 120dB/Vrms (@1kHz)
  • Frequency Range: 8Hz–40kHz (Effective: 20Hz–20kHz)
  • High-purity oxygen-free copper (OFC) silver-plated cable with 0.78mm 2-pin connectors
  • 3D-printed resin shell with matte metal faceplate
  • Harman 2019 target tuning for balanced sound

Why Choose the SIMGOT SuperMix 4?

The SIMGOT SuperMix 4 delivers exceptional audio performance with its innovative quad-driver setup, blending a 10mm dynamic driver, balanced armature, planar magnetic, and piezoelectric (PZT) drivers. Priced at $149.99, it offers punchy bass, clear mids, and detailed treble, making it ideal for gaming and music. Its low impedance and high sensitivity ensure easy pairing with smartphones or DACs, providing a cohesive and engaging soundstage.

More Details:

The SIMGOT SuperMix 4 excels in gaming with precise imaging and a wide soundstage, allowing users to pinpoint in-game sounds like footsteps or gunfire with ease, as noted in reviews for games like PUBG and Valorant. The 10mm dynamic driver delivers deep, controlled sub-bass, while the balanced armature ensures lush, natural mids for vocals and instruments. The planar and PZT drivers extend treble with clarity and airiness, avoiding sibilance for fatigue-free listening.

The IEM’s ergonomic 3D-printed resin shell ensures comfort during long sessions, with excellent passive noise isolation for noisy environments. The detachable OFC silver-plated cable enhances signal clarity and supports upgrades. While the accessory package (three pairs of silicone eartips, carrying case) is basic, the SuperMix 4’s technical performance—including detail retrieval and instrument separation—rivals higher-tier IEMs, making it a standout choice for its price.
