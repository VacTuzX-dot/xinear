Tanchjim Origin

A highly versatile single dynamic driver IEM, ideal for audiophiles and music enthusiasts seeking a mid-centric, natural sound experience.

Key Features:

  • 10mm DMT5 dual-magnetic dual-cavity dynamic driver
  • Impedance: 16Ω ±5% (@1kHz)
  • Sensitivity: 126dB/Vrms (@1kHz)
  • Frequency Response: 2Hz–48kHz
  • Ultra-low distortion: THD+N <0.056% (@1kHz, 94dB)
  • Three replaceable tuning nozzles (S-Standard, D-Dynamic, L-Light)
  • High-purity 6N oxygen-free copper silver-plated cable (3.5mm single-ended)
  • 304 stainless steel shells with mirror finish

The Tanchjim Origin, priced at $259, delivers exceptional audio clarity with its 5th-generation DMT dynamic driver, offering a balanced, mid-focused sound signature. More Details:

The Tanchjim Origin features a 10mm DMT5 dynamic driver with a composite diaphragm, designed for high magnetic flux, minimal distortion, and fast transient response, resulting in natural timbre and precise imaging. The three tuning nozzles allow subtle adjustments to treble and overall tonality, though differences are minimal, with the S-Standard nozzle offering the best balance for most users. Its soundstage is wide and open, with excellent resolution and vocal-forward mids, making it ideal for acoustic, jazz, and classical genres, as noted in Headfonics and Headfonia reviews.

The ergonomic design, crafted from 304 stainless steel with a mirror finish, ensures durability and a comfortable fit, enhanced by Tanchjim’s T-APB ear tips for superior isolation and reduced ear canal pressure. The included 6N OFC silver-plated cable is lightweight but only supports 3.5mm termination, which may limit compatibility with balanced sources. Accessories include a spacious PVC leather case, six pairs of silicone ear tips, and two additional tuning nozzle sets, though the mirror finish is prone to fingerprints. The Origin is easy to drive with smartphones or dongle DACs, scaling well with high-end sources like the Lotoo Paw Gold Touch for enhanced dynamics.
